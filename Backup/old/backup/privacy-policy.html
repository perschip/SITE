Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Tristate Cards ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with your use of our website (tristatecards.com) and services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: When you set up our website, you may provide credentials for your eBay seller account and Whatnot account.
  • Contact information: If you contact us, you may provide your name, email address, and other contact details.

1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage information: We collect information about your interactions with our website, such as the pages you visit and the features you use.
  • Device information: We collect information about the device you use to access our website, including device type, operating system, and browser type.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Display your eBay listings on our website
  • Show your Whatnot live status
  • Respond to your requests and communicate with you
  • Analyze how our website is used
  • Prevent fraud and secure our services

3. Third-Party Services

Our website integrates with third-party services:

  • eBay: When you connect your eBay account, we access your public listing information through the eBay API. This connection is governed by eBay's privacy policy.
  • Whatnot: We check your public Whatnot profile to determine if you are currently live streaming.

4. Data Storage

We store your eBay and Whatnot credentials locally in your browser using localStorage. These credentials are only used on your device and are not transmitted to our servers. Access tokens for eBay API calls are also stored locally.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we have about you
  • Request that we delete your personal information
  • Object to the processing of your personal information
  • Withdraw your consent at any time by removing your credentials from our website

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
