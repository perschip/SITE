About Tristate Cards

Our Story

Tristate Cards was founded by passionate sports card collectors from the tri-state area. We specialize in high-quality sports cards and memorabilia, focusing on baseball, basketball, football, and other sports collectibles.

Our Mission

We aim to provide collectors with authentic, high-quality sports cards and collectibles at fair prices. Whether you're a seasoned collector or just starting your collection, we're here to help you find the perfect additions to your collection.

Where to Find Us

We sell primarily through eBay and Whatnot. Check out our live streams on Whatnot for special deals and exclusive offers!
